Icecream Recipe!

Crust:
1/2 cup of butter
1/3 cup of sugar
¾ teaspoons salt
¼ teaspoons almond extract
1 egg yolk
1-1/4 cup flour
Filling:
2 cups fresh sliced strawberries
whipped cream
1 Häagen-Dazs Mango Ice Cream
Caramel Sauce* (store bought or see below recipe to make your own)
Caramel Sauce:
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup heavy cream
4 tablespoons butter
3 teaspoons vanilla
dash salt
